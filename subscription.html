Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?
You can choose a plan that suits your needs. We'll deliver your selected essentials daily/weekly as per your schedule. You can pause, modify, or cancel anytime.
Can I change my subscription items?
Yes! You can modify your subscription items anytime before 8 PM for next day delivery. Add or remove items as per your needs.
What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel your subscription anytime. For monthly plans, the current month will be completed and no further charges will be applied.
What if I'm not home during delivery?
We'll leave it at your doorstep or with a neighbor. You can also reschedule delivery through our app.
